Insights and Visualization Report¶

Tumininu Sosanya¶

A visualization report about the weratedogs wrangle project which was gathered from three datasets and assessed and cleaned into one dataframe.

  • tweet_id: tweet identification number
  • timestamp: Day the tweet was tweeeted
  • source: the tweet link thats shows where the tweet was tweeted from
  • Text: the tet written in the tweet
  • name : the name of the dogs
  • dog_stage : the stage of the dog there are 4 main stage(doggo,puppo,puper,flooper)
  • rating_denominator : the rating at the top
  • rating_numerator : the rating at the top
  • retweet_count : numebers of retweets
  • favorite_count: numbers of favorites
  • j_url: the link of tweet images
  • img_num : the image number betweebn 1- 4
  • confidence : the confidence level of the image after running it through a neural network
  • predictions : the dog breeds with the highest confidence point after extracting if from the three different predictions
